Winter 2015: HS 852

Scientific Methods for Health Sciences: Linear Modeling

This course presents the general linear modeling methods focusing on commonly employed scientific computing techniques used in health sciences. The primary aim of the course is to provide students with the necessary skills to determine appropriate use, carry out, and interpret general linear modeling. Statistical software will be used to manipulate data, fit models and perform model diagnostics.
