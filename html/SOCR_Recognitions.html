SOCR Recognitions

SOCR V2.6 receives the Softpedia "100% Free & Clean" award. This certifies that all SOCR tools are free of all forms of malware, including spyware, viruses, trojans and backdoors, and can be used and installed with no concern by any computer user.
The entire suite of SOCR Applets is made available as part of the www.PsyResearch.org Statistics resources.
Various SOCR Tools and the SOCR EBook were included in the Engineering Pathway NEEDS library.
Nicolas Christou, SOCR Co-Director, was awarded the prestigious Brian P. Copenhaver Award for Innovation in Teaching with Technology for 2008. More information is available here.
